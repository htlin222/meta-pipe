# Eligibility Criteria

**Study Title:** Comparative Efficacy and Safety of Treatment Strategies Following Progression on CDK4/6 Inhibitors in HR+/HER2- Metastatic Breast Cancer: A Systematic Review and Network Meta-Analysis

**Protocol Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2026-02-06

---

## 1. Population (P)

### Inclusion Criteria

**Patient Characteristics:**

- ✅ **Confirmed HR+ (ER+ and/or PR+) breast cancer**
  - ER ≥1% positive by IHC (per ASCO/CAP guidelines)
  - PR positivity may vary (≥1% or ≥10% depending on trial)

- ✅ **HER2-negative status**
  - IHC 0 or 1+, OR
  - IHC 2+ with FISH/ISH non-amplified (HER2/CEP17 ratio <2.0 or HER2 gene copy number <4.0)

- ✅ **Advanced or metastatic breast cancer**
  - Locally advanced unresectable (stage IIIB or IIIC), OR
  - Metastatic disease (stage IV)

- ✅ **Prior CDK4/6 inhibitor therapy**
  - Must have received ≥1 dose of palbociclib, ribociclib, or abemaciclib
  - Given in combination with endocrine therapy (AI, fulvestrant, or tamoxifen)
  - In advanced/metastatic setting (not adjuvant)

- ✅ **Disease progression on or after CDK4/6 inhibitor**
  - Radiographic progression per RECIST v1.1, OR
  - Clinical progression (symptomatic deterioration, new lesions)
  - Progression during treatment or within 12 months of last dose

- ✅ **Age ≥18 years**

- ✅ **Any ECOG performance status (0-4)**
  - Studies restricted to ECOG 0-2 are acceptable

- ✅ **Measurable or non-measurable disease**
  - Per RECIST v1.1 criteria

### Exclusion Criteria

**Patient Characteristics:**

- ❌ **HER2-positive breast cancer**
  - IHC 3+, OR
  - IHC 2+ with FISH/ISH amplified

- ❌ **Triple-negative breast cancer (TNBC)**
  - ER <1%, PR <1%, HER2-negative

- ❌ **CDK4/6 inhibitor-naïve patients**
  - Patients who never received CDK4/6 inhibitor therapy

- ❌ **Early-stage breast cancer (adjuvant/neoadjuvant setting only)**
  - Studies exclusively in stage I-III disease without metastatic component
  - Exception: Patients who progressed to metastatic disease after adjuvant CDK4/6i (if clearly reported)

- ❌ **Male breast cancer**
  - Unless trial explicitly reports outcomes stratified by sex
  - Or unless male patients represent <5% of study population

## 2. Intervention (I)

### Included Treatment Strategies

#### **Category 1: CDK4/6 Inhibitor Continuation/Switching**

- Switch to different CDK4/6 inhibitor + endocrine therapy
- Continue same CDK4/6 inhibitor with different endocrine partner
- **Examples:**
  - Abemaciclib + fulvestrant after palbociclib failure
  - Ribociclib + ET after palbociclib/abemaciclib failure

#### **Category 2: mTOR Inhibitors**

- Everolimus + exemestane
- Everolimus + fulvestrant
- Other mTOR inhibitors (temsirolimus, ridaforolimus) if studied

#### **Category 3: Antibody-Drug Conjugates (ADCs)**

- Sacituzumab govitecan (anti-TROP2 ADC)
- Datopotamab deruxtecan (anti-TROP2 ADC)
- Trastuzumab deruxtecan (if studied in HER2-low population after CDK4/6i)

#### **Category 4: Novel Endocrine Therapies**

- Oral SERDs:
  - Elacestrant
  - Imlunestrant
  - Camizestrant
  - Giredestrant
- Oral SERDs + CDK4/6 inhibitor combination
- Other novel endocrine agents (PROTACs, CERANs)

#### **Category 5: PI3K/AKT/mTOR Pathway Inhibitors**

- AKT inhibitors:
  - Capivasertib + fulvestrant
  - Ipatasertib + fulvestrant
- PI3K inhibitors (PIK3CA-mutated population):
  - Alpelisib + fulvestrant
  - Inavolisib + fulvestrant
- Dual mTORC1/2 inhibitors:
  - Sapanisertib + fulvestrant

#### **Category 6: Chemotherapy**

- Single-agent chemotherapy:
  - Capecitabine
  - Paclitaxel (weekly or every 3 weeks)
  - Docetaxel
  - Nab-paclitaxel
  - Eribulin
  - Vinorelbine
  - Gemcitabine
- Combination chemotherapy:
  - Carboplatin + gemcitabine
  - Carboplatin + paclitaxel
  - Other standard combinations

### Exclusion Criteria for Interventions

- ❌ **Treatments given before CDK4/6 inhibitor progression**
  - E.g., studies of CDK4/6i + ET in CDK4/6i-naïve patients

- ❌ **Investigational agents without Phase II/III data**
  - Phase I dose-escalation studies

- ❌ **Radiation therapy or surgery as primary intervention**
  - Unless combined with systemic therapy

- ❌ **Best supportive care alone (without active systemic therapy)**
  - May be included as comparator arm

## 3. Comparator (C)

### Included Comparators

- ✅ **Endocrine therapy alone**
  - Fulvestrant monotherapy
  - Aromatase inhibitor monotherapy
  - Tamoxifen monotherapy

- ✅ **Physician's choice chemotherapy**
  - As defined in trial protocols

- ✅ **Standard of care**
  - Per trial definition

- ✅ **Placebo + endocrine therapy**
  - In placebo-controlled RCTs

- ✅ **Other active treatments in Category 1-6**
  - For network meta-analysis indirect comparisons

### Exclusion Criteria for Comparators

- ❌ **No comparator (single-arm trials)**
  - Unless external control from other trials (with careful justification)

- ❌ **Comparators not relevant to post-CDK4/6 setting**

## 4. Outcomes (O)

### Primary Outcome (Must Report ≥1)

- ✅ **Progression-Free Survival (PFS)**
  - Time from randomization to disease progression (RECIST v1.1) or death
  - Reported as Hazard Ratio (HR) with 95% CI
  - Or median PFS with Kaplan-Meier curves for HR extraction

### Secondary Outcomes (Preferably Report ≥1)

- ✅ **Overall Survival (OS)**
  - Time from randomization to death from any cause
  - HR with 95% CI or median OS with KM curves

- ✅ **Objective Response Rate (ORR)**
  - Proportion with CR or PR per RECIST v1.1
  - Risk Ratio (RR) or raw counts

- ✅ **Clinical Benefit Rate (CBR)**
  - CR + PR + SD ≥24 weeks (or ≥6 months)

- ✅ **Safety Outcomes:**
  - Grade 3-4 adverse events (any, or specific)
  - Treatment discontinuation due to AEs
  - Treatment-related deaths

- ✅ **Quality of Life (QoL)**
  - EORTC QLQ-C30, FACT-B, or other validated scales

### Exclusion Criteria for Outcomes

- ❌ **No survival or response data**
  - Studies reporting only safety/toxicity without efficacy

- ❌ **Median follow-up <3 months**
  - Too short for meaningful efficacy assessment

## 5. Study Design (S)

### Included Study Designs

- ✅ **Phase II Randomized Controlled Trials (RCTs)**
  - Randomized, parallel-group design
  - Minimum 2 arms

- ✅ **Phase III Randomized Controlled Trials (RCTs)**
  - Registration-enabling trials

- ✅ **Post-hoc analyses of major RCTs**
  - Subgroup analyses of post-CDK4/6 populations from larger trials
  - E.g., BOLERO-2 subgroup analysis (everolimus in post-CDK4/6 patients)

- ✅ **Large prospective cohort studies (n ≥100)**
  - For real-world evidence
  - Must have comparator group
  - Low risk of bias (per Cochrane ROBINS-I or similar tool)

### Exclusion Criteria for Study Design

- ❌ **Phase I dose-escalation studies**

- ❌ **Single-arm trials without external control**
  - Unless part of historical control analysis with robust matching

- ❌ **Case reports and case series (n <30)**

- ❌ **Retrospective studies with high risk of bias**
  - No adjustment for confounding
  - High missing data (>20%)
  - Selection bias evident

- ❌ **Conference abstracts without full-text publication**
  - Unless essential data can be obtained from trial authors or trial registry
  - Abstracts >2 years old without full publication excluded

- ❌ **Non-comparative studies**
  - Descriptive studies without control group

- ❌ **Studies with overlapping populations**
  - If multiple publications from same trial, use most recent/complete data
  - If real-world studies from overlapping databases, exclude duplicates

## 7. Subgroup Eligibility

Studies will be stratified by the following subgroups **if data are available**:

### 7.1 Prior CDK4/6 Inhibitor Type

- Palbociclib
- Ribociclib
- Abemaciclib

### 7.2 Endocrine Sensitivity

- **Endocrine-sensitive:** Progression ≥12 months after starting endocrine therapy
- **Endocrine-resistant:** Progression <12 months or while on endocrine therapy

### 7.3 CDK4/6 Inhibitor Treatment Line

- First-line CDK4/6 inhibitor failure
- Second-line or later CDK4/6 inhibitor failure

### 7.4 Visceral Metastases

- Visceral disease present (liver, lung, brain, other)
- Non-visceral disease only (bone, lymph nodes, soft tissue)

### 7.5 Biomarker Status

- **PIK3CA:** Mutated vs wild-type
- **ESR1:** Mutated vs wild-type
- **TROP2:** Positive vs negative/unknown

### 7.6 Endocrine Partner with CDK4/6 Inhibitor

- Aromatase inhibitor (AI)
- Fulvestrant
- Tamoxifen

### 7.7 Prior Chemotherapy in Metastatic Setting

- Chemotherapy-naïve in metastatic setting
- Prior chemotherapy for metastatic disease

## 8. Data Extraction Requirements

### Minimum Data Required for Inclusion

**Study Characteristics:**

- Trial name/acronym
- NCT number (if available)
- Publication year
- Study design (Phase II/III RCT, cohort)
- Sample size (per arm)
- Median follow-up duration

**Patient Characteristics:**

- Age (median or mean)
- ECOG performance status (0-1 vs ≥2)
- Prior CDK4/6 inhibitor (drug name, line of therapy)
- Endocrine sensitivity status
- Visceral metastases (%)
- Biomarker status (PIK3CA, ESR1, TROP2 if reported)

**Efficacy Outcomes (≥1 required):**

- PFS: HR with 95% CI, or median PFS with KM curve
- OS: HR with 95% CI, or median OS with KM curve
- ORR: n/N or percentage
- CBR: n/N or percentage

**Safety Outcomes (if reported):**

- Grade 3-4 AEs: n/N or percentage
- Treatment discontinuation: n/N or percentage
- Treatment-related deaths: n/N

**Risk of Bias Assessment:**

- Randomization method
- Allocation concealment
- Blinding (if applicable)
- Attrition rate
- Selective reporting

## 9. Screening Process

### Title/Abstract Screening

**Include if:**

- Population: HR+/HER2- metastatic breast cancer AND prior CDK4/6i
- Intervention: Any systemic therapy post-CDK4/6i
- Study design: RCT, post-hoc analysis, or large cohort (n ≥100)

**Exclude if:**

- Clearly ineligible population (HER2+, TNBC, early-stage only)
- No CDK4/6i exposure
- Clearly ineligible design (case report, review article, editorial)

**If uncertain:** Proceed to full-text review

### Full-Text Screening

Apply all eligibility criteria listed above (Sections 1-8)

**Dual independent review:**

- Two reviewers screen independently
- Disagreements resolved by third reviewer
- Kappa statistic calculated for agreement

---

## 10. Reasons for Exclusion (Full-Text)

Document the following exclusion reasons:

1. **Wrong population** (not HR+/HER2-, not metastatic, not post-CDK4/6i)
2. **Wrong intervention** (not a systemic therapy, given before CDK4/6i progression)
3. **Wrong comparator** (no control group, single-arm)
4. **Wrong outcomes** (no PFS/OS/ORR data, follow-up <3 months)
5. **Wrong study design** (phase I, case series, retrospective with high bias)
6. **Duplicate publication** (overlapping data with another included study)
7. **Insufficient data** (no extractable HR/RR, no KM curves, no response to author inquiry)
8. **Language** (non-English/non-Chinese without translation)
9. **Abstract only** (>2 years old, no full-text available, authors unresponsive)

## 11. Quality Assurance

### Pilot Testing

- Pilot test eligibility criteria on 10 random abstracts
- Refine criteria if Kappa <0.60
- Document all changes in protocol amendments

### Inter-Rater Reliability

- Target Kappa ≥0.60 for title/abstract screening
- Target Kappa ≥0.80 for full-text screening
- Reconcile all discrepancies before final inclusion

### Data Verification

- 10% random sample of included studies re-extracted by second reviewer
- Check for transcription errors, calculation errors
