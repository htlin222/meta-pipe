## 📊 Manual Addition Details

### 1. TROPION-Breast01 (Rugo 2024)

- **PMID:** 39265124
- **DOI:** 10.1200/JCO.24.00920
- **Population:** HR+/HER2- inoperable/metastatic BC, 1-2 prior lines of chemo, **89% had prior CDK4/6i**
- **Intervention:** Datopotamab deruxtecan 6 mg/kg Q3W vs chemotherapy (eribulin/vinorelbine/capecitabine/gemcitabine)
- **Results:** Median PFS 6.9 vs 4.9 months (HR 0.63, P<0.001)
- **OS:** Interim analysis did not reach statistical significance (HR 1.01)
- **FDA Approval:** January 2025 (Datroway)

### 2. EMERALD (Bidard 2022 + Hurvitz 2024)

- **Main paper PMID:** 35584336
- **Subgroup PMID:** 39087959
- **Population:** ER+/HER2- ABC, **100% had prior CDK4/6i**, ESR1-mutated tumors (primary endpoint)
- **Intervention:** Elacestrant 345 mg daily vs standard ET (AI or fulvestrant)
- **Results (ESR1-mutated):** Median PFS 3.8 vs 1.9 months (HR 0.55, P=0.0005)
- **Subgroup (≥12 months prior ET+CDK4/6i):** Median PFS 8.6 vs 1.9 months (HR 0.41)
- **FDA Approval:** 2023 for ER+/HER2-, ESR1-mutated ABC

### 3. SOLAR-1 (André 2021)

- **PMID:** 33246021
- **DOI:** 10.1016/j.annonc.2020.11.011
- **Population:** HR+/HER2- ABC, PIK3CA-mutated, progression on/after AI, **~6% had prior CDK4/6i**
- **Intervention:** Alpelisib 300 mg + fulvestrant vs placebo + fulvestrant
- **Results (PIK3CA-mutated):** Median OS 39.3 vs 31.4 months (HR 0.86, P=0.15, did not meet OS endpoint)
- **Subgroup (liver/lung mets):** OS HR 0.68 (0.46-1.00)
- **Note:** Trial conducted 2015-2019 before CDK4/6i became standard

### 4. BOLERO-2 Post-CDK4/6i Studies

**Fernandez 2021 (The Oncologist):**

- **PMID:** 33373062
- **Real-world study:** 109 patients with HR+ MBC treated with everolimus + exemestane, 27% had prior CDK4/6i
- **Results:** Median PFS 4.2 months (prior CDK4/6i) vs 5.4 months (no prior CDK4/6i), HR 1.46, P=0.16
- **Conclusion:** Prior CDK4/6i did not significantly impact PFS/OS, though results lower than BOLERO-2

**Schettini 2020 (Anticancer Research):**

- **PMID:** 32620642
- **Retrospective study:** 32 HR+/HER2- MBC patients progressing after ≥6 months on palbociclib
- **Results:** Median PFS on everolimus 4.3 months (95% CI 2.3-6.2), CBR 56.2%
- **Conclusion:** Modest activity post-palbociclib failure
