# Meta-Analysis Research Topic

## Title (English)
Comparative Efficacy and Safety of Treatment Strategies Following Progression on CDK4/6 Inhibitors in Hormone Receptor-Positive, HER2-Negative Metastatic Breast Cancer: A Systematic Review and Network Meta-Analysis

## Title (中文)
HR+/HER2- 轉移性乳癌患者在 CDK4/6 抑制劑治療失敗後不同治療策略的療效與安全性比較：系統性回顧與網絡統合分析

---

## PICO Framework

### Population (研究對象)
**Inclusion:**
- Patients with hormone receptor-positive (HR+), HER2-negative (HER2-) advanced or metastatic breast cancer
- Disease progression on or after CDK4/6 inhibitor therapy (palbociclib, ribociclib, or abemaciclib) in combination with endocrine therapy
- Age ≥18 years
- Any ECOG performance status
- Both endocrine-sensitive and endocrine-resistant disease

**Exclusion:**
- HER2-positive breast cancer
- Triple-negative breast cancer
- Early-stage breast cancer (adjuvant/neoadjuvant setting)
- Male breast cancer (unless explicitly stratified)

---

### Intervention (介入措施)

**Primary Treatment Strategies:**

1. **CDK4/6 Inhibitor Continuation/Switching:**
   - Switch to different CDK4/6 inhibitor + endocrine therapy
   - Example trials: postMONARCH, MAINTAIN, PALMIRA

2. **mTOR Inhibitors:**
   - Everolimus + exemestane or fulvestrant
   - Example trials: BOLERO-2 (post-CDK4/6 subgroup)

3. **Antibody-Drug Conjugates (ADCs):**
   - Sacituzumab govitecan
   - Datopotamab deruxtecan
   - Example trials: TROPiCS-02, TROPION-Breast01

4. **Novel Endocrine Therapy:**
   - Oral SERDs (elacestrant, imlunestrant)
   - SERD + CDK4/6 inhibitor combination
   - Example trials: EMBER-3, EMERALD

5. **PI3K/AKT/mTOR Pathway Inhibitors:**
   - Capivasertib + fulvestrant
   - Alpelisib + fulvestrant (PIK3CA-mutated)
   - Sapanisertib + fulvestrant
   - Example trials: CAPItello-291, SOLAR-1

6. **Chemotherapy:**
   - Single-agent chemotherapy (capecitabine, taxanes, eribulin)
   - Combination chemotherapy

---

### Comparator (對照組)
- Network meta-analysis: Indirect comparisons between all treatment strategies
- Direct comparisons where head-to-head trials exist
- Standard of care (physician's choice or best supportive care)

---

### Outcomes (結果指標)

**Primary Outcome:**
- **Progression-Free Survival (PFS)** - time from randomization to disease progression or death

**Secondary Outcomes:**
1. **Overall Survival (OS)** - time from randomization to death from any cause
2. **Objective Response Rate (ORR)** - proportion achieving complete or partial response
3. **Clinical Benefit Rate (CBR)** - ORR + stable disease ≥24 weeks
4. **Safety:**
   - Grade 3-4 adverse events (overall and specific: neutropenia, diarrhea, hepatotoxicity, QTc prolongation)
   - Treatment discontinuation due to adverse events
   - Treatment-related deaths
5. **Quality of Life (QoL)** - if reported (EORTC QLQ-C30, FACT-B)

---

## Subgroup Analyses (亞群分析)

### Pre-specified Subgroups:
1. **Prior CDK4/6 Inhibitor Type:**
   - Palbociclib vs Ribociclib vs Abemaciclib

2. **Endocrine Sensitivity:**
   - Endocrine-sensitive disease (progression ≥12 months after starting ET)
   - Endocrine-resistant disease (progression <12 months or on ET)

3. **CDK4/6 Inhibitor Treatment Line:**
   - First-line CDK4/6 inhibitor failure
   - Second-line CDK4/6 inhibitor failure

4. **Presence of Visceral Metastases:**
   - Visceral disease vs non-visceral disease

5. **Biomarker Status (if available):**
   - PIK3CA mutation status
   - ESR1 mutation status
   - TROP2 expression

6. **Type of Endocrine Partner:**
   - Aromatase inhibitor vs Fulvestrant

## Study Design

### Study Types to Include:
- **Phase II/III randomized controlled trials (RCTs)** - primary evidence
- **Post-hoc analyses** of major trials in post-CDK4/6 populations
- **Large prospective cohort studies** (n ≥100) for real-world evidence

### Exclusion:
- Case reports, case series (n <30)
- Retrospective studies with high risk of bias
- Conference abstracts without full-text publication (unless essential data unavailable elsewhere)

## Risk of Bias Assessment

**Tool:** Cochrane Risk of Bias 2 (RoB 2) for RCTs
- Randomization process
- Deviations from intended interventions
- Missing outcome data
- Measurement of the outcome
- Selection of the reported result

---

## Data Analysis Plan

### Statistical Methods:

1. **Network Meta-Analysis (NMA):**
   - Bayesian hierarchical model using frequentist approach
   - Software: R (netmeta package) or Stata (network meta)
   - Rank treatments using P-score or SUCRA

2. **Heterogeneity Assessment:**
   - I² statistic (>50% = substantial heterogeneity)
   - τ² (between-study variance)
   - Prediction intervals

3. **Sensitivity Analyses:**
   - Exclude high risk-of-bias studies
   - Exclude studies with <6 months follow-up
   - Fixed-effect vs random-effects models

4. **Publication Bias:**
   - Funnel plots (if ≥10 studies)
   - Egger's test
   - Trim-and-fill method

---

## Clinical Significance

### Rationale:
- **>50% of patients** progress on CDK4/6 inhibitors within 2 years
- **Lack of head-to-head trials** comparing post-CDK4/6 treatment strategies
- **Rapidly evolving landscape** with new ADCs and oral SERDs
- **Clinical equipoise** - physicians need evidence-based guidance

### Expected Impact:
- Inform treatment sequencing guidelines
- Support regulatory/reimbursement decisions
- Identify patient subgroups most likely to benefit from specific strategies

---

## Key Trials to Include

### Confirmed Trials:
1. **postMONARCH** (abemaciclib + fulvestrant vs fulvestrant alone)
2. **MAINTAIN** (ribociclib continuation)
3. **PALMIRA** (palbociclib continuation - negative trial)
4. **TROPiCS-02** (sacituzumab govitecan vs chemotherapy)
5. **TROPION-Breast01** (datopotamab deruxtecan vs chemotherapy)
6. **EMBER-3** (imlunestrant ± abemaciclib)
7. **EMERALD** (elacestrant vs standard ET)
8. **CAPItello-291** (capivasertib + fulvestrant)
9. **SOLAR-1** (alpelisib + fulvestrant, PIK3CA-mutated)
10. **BOLERO-2** (everolimus + exemestane - subgroup analysis)
