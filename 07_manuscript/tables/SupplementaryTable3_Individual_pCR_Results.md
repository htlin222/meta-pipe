## Individual Trial Results Commentary

### KEYNOTE-522 (Largest Trial, 49% of Total Weight)

- **Strengths**: Largest sample (N=1174), longest follow-up (75.1 months), only trial with adjuvant ICI, reported OS benefit
- **pCR Findings**: Absolute benefit +13.6% (64.8% vs 51.2%), highly significant (p<0.001)
- **Survival**: EFS HR 0.65 (p<0.001), OS HR 0.66 (p=0.0015), validating pCR as surrogate endpoint
- **Unique Feature**: 2:1 randomization to maximize ICI exposure

### IMpassion031 (Second Largest Trial)

- **Strengths**: Double-blind, Phase 3, mature EFS data (39 months)
- **pCR Findings**: Absolute benefit +16.5% (57.6% vs 41.1%), significant (p=0.0044)
- **Survival**: EFS HR 0.76 (not statistically significant individually)
- **Unique Feature**: Used atezolizumab (PD-L1 inhibitor) with nab-paclitaxel without platinum

### GeparNuevo (Phase 2 Trial)

- **Strengths**: Only trial using durvalumab, reported OS data (36 months)
- **pCR Findings**: Absolute benefit +9.2% (53.4% vs 44.2%), not significant (p=0.224)
- **Survival**: EFS HR 0.54 (p=0.056, borderline), OS HR 0.26 (p=0.0076, significant)
- **Limitation**: Smallest sample (N=174), Phase 2 design, ypT0 ypN0 definition (more stringent)

### NeoTRIPaPDL1 (Only Open-Label Trial)

- **Strengths**: Used carboplatin with nab-paclitaxel throughout taxane phase
- **pCR Findings**: Absolute benefit +4.2% (48.6% vs 44.4%), not significant (p=0.48)
- **Limitation**: Open-label design (some concerns for bias), no survival data yet, lowest pCR rates
- **Note**: Shortest chemotherapy duration (17-18 weeks vs 24 weeks for others)

### CamRelief (Most Recent Trial)

- **Strengths**: Used camrelizumab (first PD-1 inhibitor from China), double-blind Phase 3
- **pCR Findings**: Absolute benefit +12.1% (56.8% vs 44.7%), significant (p=0.004)
- **Limitation**: Shortest follow-up (14.4 months), no survival data yet
- **Unique Feature**: Only other trial using carboplatin throughout (like KEYNOTE-522)

## Meta-Analysis Methodology

**Statistical Model**: Random-effects meta-analysis using Mantel-Haenszel method for risk ratios

**Heterogeneity Assessment**: I² = 0% (no heterogeneity), τ² = 0, Cochran's Q = 1.34 (p=0.85)

**Interpretation of I²=0%**: Remarkably consistent treatment effect across trials despite:

- Different ICI agents (3 PD-1, 2 PD-L1 inhibitors)
- Different chemotherapy regimens (with/without platinum)
- Different pCR definitions (ypT0/Tis vs ypT0 only)
- Different patient populations (ER <1% vs <10%)
- Different trial phases (Phase 2 vs Phase 3)

This consistency suggests a robust **class effect** of ICI across neoadjuvant TNBC trials.

**Publication Bias**: Egger's test p=0.713 (no evidence of small-study effects or publication bias)

**Sensitivity Analysis**: Leave-one-out analysis showed stable pooled estimates:

- Excluding KEYNOTE-522: RR 1.26 (1.10–1.44)
- Excluding IMpassion031: RR 1.25 (1.14–1.36)
- Excluding GeparNuevo: RR 1.27 (1.16–1.38)
- Excluding NeoTRIPaPDL1: RR 1.28 (1.17–1.40)
- Excluding CamRelief: RR 1.26 (1.14–1.39)

Range: RR 1.25–1.28 (very narrow, indicating robustness)
