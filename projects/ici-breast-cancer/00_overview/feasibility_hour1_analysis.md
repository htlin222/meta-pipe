## Detailed Study Review (15 abstracts)

### Key RCTs Identified for Inclusion:

1. **KEYNOTE-522** (Schmid et al. 2020-2024) - THE LANDMARK TRIAL
   - **Design**: Phase III RCT, n=1174
   - **Intervention**: Pembrolizumab + carboplatin + paclitaxel → AC
   - **Comparator**: Placebo + same chemotherapy
   - **Outcomes reported**:
     - pCR: ✅ Reported (#1)
     - EFS: ✅ Reported (#3)
     - OS: ✅ Reported (#2)
   - **Sample size**: Large (n=1174)
   - **Assessment**: ⭐⭐⭐ MUST INCLUDE - Gold standard study

2. **IMpassion031** (Mittendorf et al. 2020)
   - **Design**: Phase III RCT, early TNBC
   - **Intervention**: Atezolizumab + nab-paclitaxel → doxorubicin + cyclophosphamide
   - **Comparator**: Placebo + same chemotherapy
   - **Outcomes**: pCR ✅
   - **Assessment**: ⭐⭐⭐ MUST INCLUDE

3. **GeparNuevo** (Loibl et al. 2022)
   - **Design**: Phase II RCT (NCT02685059)
   - **Intervention**: Durvalumab + NACT
   - **Outcomes**: pCR, survival ✅
   - **Key finding**: Survival benefit independent of pCR
   - **Assessment**: ⭐⭐⭐ MUST INCLUDE

4. **CamRelief** (Chen et al. 2025)
   - **Design**: RCT, early/locally advanced TNBC
   - **Intervention**: Camrelizumab (PD-1 inhibitor) + chemotherapy
   - **Comparator**: Placebo + chemotherapy
   - **Outcomes**: pCR ✅
   - **Assessment**: ⭐⭐⭐ MUST INCLUDE (Chinese PD-1 inhibitor data)

5. **NeoPACT** (Sharma et al. 2024)
   - **Design**: Phase II RCT
   - **Intervention**: Pembrolizumab + carboplatin + docetaxel (anthracycline-free)
   - **Outcomes**: pCR, EFS ✅
   - **Assessment**: ⭐⭐ LIKELY INCLUDE (anthracycline-free regimen)

### Studies for EXCLUSION (not neoadjuvant TNBC):

6. **KEYNOTE-355** (#5) - ❌ EXCLUDE
   - Reason: Metastatic TNBC, not early/neoadjuvant

7. **IMpassion130** (#7) - ❌ EXCLUDE
   - Reason: Advanced TNBC, not neoadjuvant

8. **IMpassion131** (#12) - ❌ EXCLUDE
   - Reason: Advanced/metastatic TNBC, not neoadjuvant

9. **ER+/HER2- study** (#14) - ❌ EXCLUDE
   - Reason: NOT triple-negative (ER-positive)

10. **TROPION-Breast03** (#13) - ❌ EXCLUDE (for now)

    - Reason: Basic science, not an RCT
