# Table 1. Characteristics of Included Trials

| Trial            | NCT Number  | First Author (Year) | Design      | N Total | N ICI | N Control | Randomization | Blinding     | ICI Agent (Dose, Schedule) | Chemotherapy Backbone                        | Median Follow-up (months) | Outcomes Reported |
| ---------------- | ----------- | ------------------- | ----------- | ------- | ----- | --------- | ------------- | ------------ | -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- | ------------------------- | ----------------- |
| **KEYNOTE-522**  | NCT03036488 | Schmid (2024)       | Phase 3 RCT | 1174    | 784   | 390       | 2:1           | Double-blind | Pembrolizumab (200mg Q3W)  | Paclitaxel + carboplatin → AC                | 75.1                      | pCR, EFS, OS      |
| **IMpassion031** | NCT03197935 | Mittendorf (2025)   | Phase 3 RCT | 333     | 165   | 168       | 1:1           | Double-blind | Atezolizumab (840mg Q2W)   | Nab-paclitaxel → AC                          | 39.0                      | pCR, EFS          |
| **GeparNuevo**   | NCT02685059 | Loibl (2022)        | Phase 2 RCT | 174     | 88    | 86        | 1:1           | Double-blind | Durvalumab (1500mg Q4W)    | Nab-paclitaxel → EC                          | 36.0                      | pCR, EFS, OS      |
| **NeoTRIPaPDL1** | NCT02620280 | Gianni (2022)       | Phase 3 RCT | 280     | 138   | 142       | 1:1           | Open-label   | Atezolizumab (1200mg Q3W)  | Carboplatin + nab-paclitaxel → anthracycline | 54.0                      | pCR               |
| **CamRelief**    | NCT04613674 | Chen (2025)         | Phase 3 RCT | 441     | 222   | 219       | 1:1           | Double-blind | Camrelizumab (200mg Q2W)   | Nab-paclitaxel + carboplatin → EC            | 14.4                      | pCR               |

**Abbreviations**: AC, doxorubicin/cyclophosphamide; EC, epirubicin/cyclophosphamide; EFS, event-free survival; ICI, immune checkpoint inhibitor; OS, overall survival; pCR, pathologic complete response; Q2W, every 2 weeks; Q3W, every 3 weeks; Q4W, every 4 weeks; RCT, randomized controlled trial.

**Notes**:

- All trials enrolled patients with early-stage or locally advanced triple-negative breast cancer (TNBC) defined as ER<1%, PR<1%, HER2-negative.
- pCR definition: ypT0/Tis ypN0 (absence of invasive cancer in breast and axillary lymph nodes) in all trials except GeparNuevo and NeoTRIPaPDL1 (ypT0 ypN0, excluding Tis).
- ICI was administered during neoadjuvant phase only in IMpassion031, GeparNuevo, NeoTRIPaPDL1, and CamRelief; KEYNOTE-522 continued pembrolizumab for up to 9 cycles adjuvantly.
- Total N=2402 patients across all 5 trials.
