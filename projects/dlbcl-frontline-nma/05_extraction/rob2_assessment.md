1. **ECOG-E1412**: Open-label design; potential for performance bias, though PFS/OS are objective endpoints

## Domain Details

### D1: Randomization Process
- All trials used centralized randomization with stratification by IPI, COO, or region
- ROBUST and PHOENIX were double-blind (placebo-controlled)
- POLARIX: open-label but stratified by IPI and region

### D2: Deviations from Intended Interventions
- PHOENIX: Higher treatment discontinuation in ibrutinib arm for age ≥60 (toxicity-driven)
- CALGB 50303: DA-EPOCH-R requires dose adjustments by design (not a protocol deviation)
- All others: Standard chemotherapy protocols with expected deviation rates

### D3: Missing Outcome Data
- All trials analyzed ITT population
- Loss to follow-up <5% across all trials
- Sensitivity analyses with per-protocol populations reported in most trials

### D4: Measurement of the Outcome
- PFS assessed by investigator in most trials (POLARIX also had IRC)
- OS is objective and unaffected by assessment bias
- CR assessed by PET-CT (Lugano or Cheson criteria)

### D5: Selection of the Reported Result
- All trials pre-registered with primary endpoints specified
- All reported according to pre-specified statistical analysis plans
- PHOENIX switched from EFS to PFS reporting in some analyses — documented
