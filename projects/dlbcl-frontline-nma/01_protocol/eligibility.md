# Eligibility Criteria

## Inclusion Criteria

1. **Population**: Adults (≥18 years) with previously untreated, histologically confirmed DLBCL (de novo)
2. **Intervention**: Any novel frontline chemoimmunotherapy regimen (added or substituted agent vs standard backbone)
3. **Comparator**: R-CHOP or R-CHOP-like regimen as control arm
4. **Outcomes**: Reports at least one of: PFS (HR), OS (HR), CR rate, or Grade ≥3 AE rate
5. **Study design**: Phase III randomized controlled trial
6. **Publication**: Full-text article, conference abstract with sufficient quantitative data (HR + 95% CI), or regulatory submission data

## Exclusion Criteria

1. Phase I or Phase II trials (regardless of sample size)
2. Single-arm studies without a randomized comparator
3. Retrospective or observational studies
4. Trials exclusively enrolling relapsed/refractory DLBCL
5. Trials exclusively enrolling primary mediastinal B-cell lymphoma (PMBCL)
6. Trials exclusively enrolling transformed indolent lymphoma
7. Trials with non-chemotherapy-based arms only (e.g., lenalidomide monotherapy)
8. Duplicate publications (use most mature data; document in decision log)
9. Trials with <50 patients per arm
10. Trials not reporting any extractable efficacy data

## Special Considerations

- **ROBUST trial**: Enrolled ABC-subtype DLBCL only — include in overall NMA but flag population restriction; use for COO subgroup analysis
- **Multiple publications from same trial**: Use most recent/mature data (e.g., POLARIX 5-year > 2-year)
- **Conference abstracts**: Include only if HR + 95% CI are reported; flag as abstract-only in quality assessment
