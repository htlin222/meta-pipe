# Screening Process

## Overview
- **Total records from PubMed search**: 194
- **Screening method**: Two-phase systematic screening
  - Phase 1: Title/abstract screening against eligibility criteria
  - Phase 2: Full-text verification of potentially eligible studies

## Eligibility Criteria Applied
- Phase III RCT comparing novel regimen vs R-CHOP in untreated DLBCL
- Reports PFS or OS as HR with 95% CI
- ≥50 patients per arm

## Exclusion Categories
| Code | Reason | Count |
|------|--------|-------|
