## Eligibility Criteria Applied

Full-text screening re-evaluated each study against PICO criteria:

- **Population**: Adult cancer patients receiving myelosuppressive chemotherapy
- **Intervention**: G-CSF prophylaxis (filgrastim, pegfilgrastim, lipegfilgrastim, or biosimilar)
- **Comparator**: Placebo, no G-CSF, or another G-CSF agent (head-to-head)
- **Outcome**: Febrile neutropenia (FN) incidence, severe neutropenia (grade 3/4), or related endpoints
- **Design**: Randomized controlled trial (RCT), including phase II and phase III

## Detailed Decisions

All 18 studies met full-text eligibility criteria. Two studies warranted additional discussion:

### Hegg2016 (Moderate Confidence)

- **Concern**: Randomized phase II design; potential for unclear comparator structure.
- **Resolution**: Full-text review confirmed a randomized design with a comparator arm (pegfilgrastim vs. control) reporting febrile neutropenia incidence. Both reviewers agreed the study meets PICO criteria and provides extractable comparative data for the NMA.
- **Decision**: Include

### Bozzoli2015 (Noted for Generalizability)

- **Concern**: Multiple myeloma with VTD regimen differs from the predominantly solid-tumor population.
- **Resolution**: The study is a prospective, randomized trial of pegfilgrastim prophylaxis with FN as an endpoint. Including hematologic malignancies broadens generalizability. Sensitivity analysis excluding this study is planned at the analysis stage (Stage 06) to assess impact on pooled estimates.
- **Decision**: Include

### Remaining 16 Studies

All clearly met eligibility criteria with high confidence from both reviewers. No exclusion codes assigned.
