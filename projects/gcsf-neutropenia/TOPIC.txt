G-CSF (granulocyte colony-stimulating factor) prophylaxis
in cancer chemotherapy-induced neutropenia

Population: Adult cancer patients receiving myelosuppressive chemotherapy
Intervention: G-CSF prophylaxis (filgrastim, pegfilgrastim, lipegfilgrastim)
Comparator: Placebo or no G-CSF prophylaxis
Outcomes:
  Primary: Incidence of febrile neutropenia (FN)
  Secondary:
    - Overall survival
    - Infection-related mortality
    - Hospitalization rate due to FN
    - Time to neutrophil recovery (ANC >= 0.5 x 10^9/L)
    - Adverse events (bone pain, injection site reactions)
    - Relative dose intensity of chemotherapy
Study designs: Randomized controlled trials (RCTs)
