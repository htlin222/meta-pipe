## Key Metrics

- **Search**: 4,136 records from 3 databases (PubMed, CrossRef, Semantic Scholar)
- **After dedup**: 4,007 unique records
- **Screening**: 90 studies included across 5 literature layers
- **Open access**: 53% of included studies
- **Framework**: Arksey & O'Malley (2005), PRISMA-ScR (Tricco et al., 2018)

## Five Literature Layers

1. **L1**: Why effort is aversive (opportunity cost, effort paradox)
2. **L2**: Neural metabolic cost (glutamate accumulation, glycogen depletion)
3. **L3**: Effort as aversive signal (dACC/EVC model)
4. **L4**: Predictive processing / allostatic framing (interoceptive prediction error)
5. **L5**: Pain/headache bridging (stress-hyperalgesia, TTH)

## Key Finding

No published study has jointly measured effort-based decision shifts AND somatic discomfort localization. The field is not ready for meta-analysis. A specific experiment is proposed to fill this critical gap.
