# Eligibility Criteria — Scoping Review

## Inclusion Criteria

### Population
- Healthy adults (age >= 18) OR studies with a healthy control group
- No current headache disorder diagnosis required for inclusion

### Intervention/Exposure
- Sustained cognitive task (any duration, preference >= 30 min)
- Includes: N-back, Stroop, working memory, attentional control, simulated workday, continuous performance tasks
- Also includes theoretical/review papers proposing mechanistic accounts of cognitive effort costs

### Comparator
- Rest, low-demand control, or passive condition (for empirical studies)
- Not required for theoretical papers

### Outcomes (at least one of)
- Somatic discomfort ratings during/after cognitive task
- Effort discounting or effort-based decision making
- Subjective fatigue ratings
- Head-localized discomfort
- Neuroimaging markers of effort cost (fMRI, MRS, EEG)
- Pupillometry during sustained cognitive work
- Pericranial tenderness measures

### Study Design
- Any: RCT, within-subjects, between-subjects, observational, cross-sectional
- Theoretical/conceptual papers (for framework layers L1, L3, L4)
- Narrative/systematic reviews (for mapping the field)

### Language
- English only

## Exclusion Criteria

- Studies exclusively on diagnosed headache patients (TTH, migraine) without healthy control data
- Pure pharmacological intervention studies
- Animal studies
- Pediatric populations (< 18 years)
- Studies measuring only task performance without any discomfort/fatigue/effort outcome
- Non-English full text
- Conference abstracts without sufficient methods/results detail
