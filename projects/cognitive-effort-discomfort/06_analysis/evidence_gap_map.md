## Critical Empty Cells

The following combinations represent the most important gaps for future research:

1. **Head-localized discomfort × L1-L4**: No study in the effort/metabolic/EVC/predictive processing literatures has measured head-specific discomfort during cognitive tasks. All discomfort measurement occurs in L5 (headache medicine) with clinical populations.

2. **Interoceptive accuracy × L1-L3**: No study has examined whether interoceptive sensitivity moderates the relationship between cognitive effort costs and subjective discomfort.

3. **MRS metabolites × L3-L5**: The glutamate accumulation finding (Wiehler 2022) has not been replicated or extended to other layers. No study has examined metabolic markers during cognitive tasks while simultaneously measuring effort economics or headache.

4. **Effort discounting × L4-L5**: The behavioral economics of effort (willingness to pay for reduced cognitive load) has never been studied in conjunction with predictive processing markers or headache outcomes.
