## 📋 Manual Retrieval Workflow

### Step 1: Institutional Access (Target: 30-35 PDFs)

**Tools needed**:

- University/hospital library portal
- VPN if accessing remotely
- Browser with institutional cookies

**Process**:

1. Go to library proxy portal
2. Paste DOI into resolver (e.g., `https://doi.org/[DOI]`)
3. Download PDF
4. Rename to `[RecordID].pdf`
5. Move to `/Users/htlin/meta-pipe/04_fulltext/round-01/pdfs/`
6. Update `pdf_retrieval_manifest_updated.csv`:
   - `retrieval_status = "downloaded"`
   - `retrieval_method = "Institutional access"`
   - `pdf_path = "pdfs/[RecordID].pdf"`

**Expected success rate**: ~85% for Priority 1-3, ~60% for Priority 4-5

---

### Step 2: PubMed Central (Target: +5-8 PDFs)

**For records with PMID** that failed institutional access:

1. Go to: `https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMID[PMID]/pdf/`
2. If available, download directly
3. Update manifest with `retrieval_method = "PMC"`

**PMIDs to try**:

- 39265124 (TROPION)

- 33373062 (BOLERO-2 post-CDK)

### Step 3: Publisher Direct Download (Target: +2-4 PDFs)

Some OA articles allow direct download with browser (not bot):

1. Visit DOI URL directly in browser
2. Look for "Download PDF" button
3. Complete any simple registration if required
4. Update manifest with `retrieval_method = "Publisher direct"`

**Try first for**:

- Gold OA that failed automated download
- Recent publications (2024-2025)

---

### Step 4: Author Contact (Target: +2-4 PDFs)

### Step 5: Alternative Sources (Last Resort)

**If Steps 1-4 fail**, consider:

1. **ResearchGate** - Authors often upload preprints
2. **Conference proceedings** - For abstracts (BhaveMA2024127)
3. **ClinicalTrials.gov** - Trial results may have detailed data
4. **Supplemental materials** - Sometimes more data than article
